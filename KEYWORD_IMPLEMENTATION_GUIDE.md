## SECTION 10: COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

### What NOT to Do

1. **Don't Keyword Stuff**
   - ❌ "Luxury waterfront luxury rental luxury Mallacoota luxury property"
   - ✓ "Luxury waterfront rental in Mallacoota offering premium accommodation"

2. **Don't Ignore Natural Language**
   - ❌ "Holiday rental Mallacoota luxury waterfront pet-friendly boat"
   - ✓ "Experience luxury holiday rental in Mallacoota with waterfront access"

3. **Don't Forget Mobile Users**
   - ❌ Titles longer than 50 chars (truncate on mobile)
   - ✓ Keep important keywords in first 50 characters

4. **Don't Neglect Semantic Relationships**
   - ❌ Random keyword mixing without context
   - ✓ Group related keywords: waterfront + ocean + boat + fishing

5. **Don't Overuse Single Keyword**
   - ❌ "Mallacoota" appears in every other sentence
   - ✓ Distribute across page with LSI variations

6. **Don't Forget Internal Links**
   - ❌ Pages without internal keyword-rich linking
   - ✓ 5-8 internal links per page with descriptive anchors

7. **Don't Update Without Tracking**
   - ❌ Implement keywords without measuring impact
   - ✓ Track positions and CTR before/after changes
