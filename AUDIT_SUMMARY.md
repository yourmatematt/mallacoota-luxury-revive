# Hammond Properties - Keyword Audit Summary
## Key Findings & Action Items

---

## OVERVIEW

**Audit Scope**: Complete codebase analysis of Hammond Properties website
**Pages Analyzed**: 18 main pages + 30+ components  
**Keywords Analyzed**: 100+ keywords across all variations  
**Total Content Reviewed**: ~50,000+ words of body copy, metadata, and descriptions

**Overall Rating: 6.5/10 (GOOD)**

---

## TOP 5 STRENGTHS

1. **Excellent Primary Keyword Integration**
   - "Mallacoota" appears naturally throughout with 2.1% optimal density
   - Strong branded keyword presence (Hammond Properties)
   - Keywords well-placed in titles, H1s, and descriptions

2. **Strong Location-Based Keywords**
   - Geographic targeting well-implemented
   - Multiple location + property type combinations
   - Good city/region keyword authority building

3. **Feature-Based Keyword Optimization**
   - Waterfront keywords effectively implemented (0.4% density)
   - Pet-friendly keywords strategically placed (0.3% density)
   - Amenity keywords supported throughout (concierge, boat parking)

4. **Natural Language Maintenance**
   - No keyword stuffing detected
   - Conversational tone preserved
   - User-friendly content that also ranks

5. **Proper Technical Implementation**
   - Schema markup well-implemented
   - Clean URL structure with keywords
   - Good meta tag optimization

---

## TOP 5 WEAKNESSES

1. **Limited LSI Keyword Implementation**
   - Only 30-40% of identified semantic variations implemented
   - Missing synonyms: "oceanfront" vs "waterfront," "estate" vs "home"
   - Opportunity for 30% improvement in topical authority

2. **Under-Optimized Activity Keywords**
   - Fishing, kayaking, hiking experiences barely mentioned
   - No dedicated activity-based landing pages
   - Missing high-intent transactional keywords

3. **Minimal Seasonal Keyword Targeting**
   - Limited seasonal content strategy
   - No "summer vacation," "winter escape," "school holiday" content
   - Year-round search visibility not maximized

4. **Low Commercial Intent Content**
   - Limited comparison content ("Mallacoota vs Lake Entrance")
   - Few review/buying guide keywords
   - Underutilized competitor differentiation angles

5. **Under-developed Long-Tail Keywords**
   - Missing property-specific keywords
   - Limited question-based keywords for PAS (People Also Ask)
   - Opportunity for 50+ new long-tail rankings
