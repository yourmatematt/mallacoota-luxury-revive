KEY FINDINGS SUMMARY

CRITICAL ISSUES REQUIRING IMMEDIATE ATTENTION:

1. META DESCRIPTION LENGTH VIOLATIONS
   Pages affected: 5/8
   Current characters: 161-214
   Target: 150-160
   Examples:
     - About: 214 chars (OVER by 54) - CRITICAL
     - Holiday Rentals: 185 chars (OVER by 25)
     - Discover: 181 chars (OVER by 21)
     - Contact: 172 chars (OVER by 12)
     - Homepage: 161 chars (OVER by 1)
   Impact: Text cut off on mobile (only ~130 chars shown)
   Fix effort: 30 minutes
   Expected CTR improvement: +10%

2. TITLE LENGTH VIOLATIONS
   Pages affected: 4/8
   Current characters: 69-80
   Target: 50-65
   Examples:
     - About: 80 chars (OVER by 15)
     - Contact: 74 chars (OVER by 9)
     - Properties: 69 chars (OVER by 4)
   Impact: Truncation on mobile (<50 chars shown)
   Fix effort: 20 minutes
   Expected CTR improvement: +5%

3. FRAGMENTED META TAG MANAGEMENT
   Current systems: 3 different implementations
   Risk: Conflicts during navigation, harder to maintain
   Solution: Consolidate to single component
   Fix effort: 2-3 days
   Prevents: Future bugs and technical debt

4. MISSING IMAGE ATTRIBUTES
   Pages missing: 7/8 (all except Contact)
   Missing attributes:
     - og:image:width (1200)
     - og:image:height (630)
     - og:image:alt (descriptive)
     - twitter:image:width (1200)
     - twitter:image:height (675)
     - twitter:image:alt (descriptive)
   Impact: Poor social media sharing display
   Fix effort: 15 min per page
   Expected improvement: Better social sharing

5. SCHEMA.ORG QUALITY GAPS
   Contact: 5/5 stars (best)
   BlogDetail: 4/5 stars
   Homepage: 3/5 stars
   Properties: 3/5 stars
   Holiday Rentals: 3/5 stars
   Discover: 1/5 stars (CRITICAL - no schema)
   About: 1/5 stars (CRITICAL - no Person schema)
   Average: 2.9/5 stars → Target: 5/5 stars
   Fix effort: 3-4 hours
   Expected benefit: Better rich snippets

6. PROPERTY DETAIL og:type ERROR
   Current: og:type="website"
   Should be: og:type="og.property"
   Affects: All property detail pages
   Impact: Facebook/LinkedIn won't recognize as property
   Fix effort: 5 minutes
   Expected improvement: Better social formatting

7. GEO TAGS MISSING
   Pages missing: 7/8 (only Contact has geo tags)
   Missing tags:
     - geo.region (AU-VIC)
     - geo.placename (Mallacoota)
     - geo.position (-37.5642;149.7544)
   Impact: Missed local search rankings
   Fix effort: 20 minutes
   Expected improvement: +5% local search
